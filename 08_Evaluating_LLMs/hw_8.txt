Use for LLMs in science:
    Literature Review: LLMs can help students and scientists filter through vast amounts of scientific literature to identify relevant information, and discover new insights across various domains. This is particularly useful for someone who is trying to enter a new field or trying to write a research paper.
    Specialized LLMs for different subjects.
 
Evaluation:
    Assessing the quality of LLM output by comparing it to outputs generated by humans with knowledge in the field. Comparisons to texbooks, papers etc.
    Making sure references cited by the LLM are appropriate.
